About us

Welcome to SAIS

Our journey began with a love for equestrianism and a passion for developing innovative training tools to help riders achieve their best. With the rise of AI, we saw an opportunity to enhance the training experience by providing direct guidance that helps riders improve their skills and avoid common mistakes.

About us

Our Mission:

Our mission is to revolutionize horse training by providing accessible, AI-powered guidance that helps horse owners and trainers of all experience levels improve their skills and care for their horses more effectively.

Our Vision:

We aim to build a community of riders equipped with the confidence and knowledge needed to train and excel safely and sustainably. We believe that riding skills should be accessible to everyone with a passion for horsemanship, regardless of experience level or location.
